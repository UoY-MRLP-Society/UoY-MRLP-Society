Overview

We are a campaigning and political society on campus; influenced by the Monster Raving Loony Party we support all policies too silly for serious (boring) parties to consider. We invite you to join us in supporting loony policies that have no basis in reality. We run student led events such as wellbeing activities, policy discussion and guest speakers. Meetings are publicised on social media platforms and through our regular newsletters.

Our Goals

  • Create accessible access into politics and policy
  • Satirize and add whimsy to political discussion
  • represent the ideas of the MRLP at the university of york
